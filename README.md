## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales performance data to identify trends,
evaluate product and regional performance, and extract actionable business insights.
The analysis helps stakeholders understand revenue drivers and optimize sales strategy.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales trends
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Evaluate monthly and yearly sales patterns
- Understand revenue contribution across categories
- Generate insights for business decision-making

## Approach

### Data Loading & Inspection
- Loaded structured sales data from CSV format
- Reviewed dataset structure, columns, and data types

### Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed sales distribution across regions
- Studied product-level revenue contribution
- Examined monthly and seasonal sales trends
- Identified high- and low-performing segments

### Business Insights
- Certain regions consistently outperform others
- A small number of products contribute to majority revenue
- Sales show noticeable seasonal trends
- Opportunities exist to improve underperforming regions/products

## Dataset
- File: `data/sales_data.csv`
- Contains transactional sales records including:
  - Date
  - Region
  - Product
  - Sales Amount
  - Quantity
  - Profit

## Tech Stack
- Programming: Python
- Data Analysis: Pandas, NumPy
- Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Environment: Jupyter Notebook (Anaconda)
